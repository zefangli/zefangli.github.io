#### Areas of Expertise

_Experimental_

- Synthesis of 3D-printing polymers (especially thiol-ene systems) and liquid metals
- Material characterization:
  - Differential scanning calorimetry
  - Dynamic mechanical analysis
  - Thermogravimetric analysis
  - Nanoindentation
  - Scanning electron microscopy & energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy
  - Micro-computed tomography
  - Rheometry
  - Material testing (Instron, MTS)
- Fabrication:
  - 3D printing — direct ink writing (Aerotech, Hyrel, Voltera) and vat photopolymerization (Phrozen, Prusa, Formlabs, Objet)
  - Soft lithography, manual fabrication

_Computational_

- Geometry processing: path planning and slicing algorithms, thermal simulation for 3D printing
- Deep learning: PyTorch
- CAD/CAM/FEA: SOLIDWORKS, COMSOL, ANSYS, EAGLE
- Programming: Python, MATLAB, C/C++
